# Greatdori! & Privacy
Greatdori! is designed to protect your information and let you choose what you share.

- We collect your personal data so that we can provide you the related contents.
- To help identify and prevent fraud, Greatdori! App and connected third-party services may collect related internet connection information. This kind of information only keeps during the connection.
- To find ways to improve the services, Greatdori! and connected third-party services may collect related analysis data. This kind of data isn’t linked to you.

Greatdori! Development Team is committed to the privacy and security of your information.
We collect only the data needed to provide a better experience for you.
When we collect data, we believe it’s important for you to know what we are collecting and why we need it.
Here are some examples of the types of information we may collect and use.

## Providing Information
We use your personal data to provide related services and features in Greatdori!.
This information includes, for example, your internet connection data and the contents you view.
This kind of data isn’t linked to you, and only keeps during the connection.

## Improving Services
To improve and optimize the experience in the Greatdori! App and connected third-party services,
we and trusted third-parties collect information about your usage of the Greatdori! App,
including App settings, IP address, and contents you view and search for.
This kind of data isn’t linked to you.

## Advertisements
Greatdori! will not sell your privacy data in any way, and will not use your data for commercial use.
Greatdori! will not give your information to advertizer, and will not provide any personaled advertizements.

## Notifications
You can enable notifications in the Greatdori! App manually.
If you choose to enable notifications, we will collect a device identifier for pushing notifications.
This identifier isn’t linked to you by other methods.
You can disable notifications any time.
Once you disable notifications, we will remove the collected identifier.
